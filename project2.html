TITLE: Movie Ticket Booking Website System

Description: The project objective is to book cinema tickets online. The Ticket Reservation System is an Internet-based application that can be accessed by anyone with an internet connection. Users are required to log in to the system and need a credit card for booking tickets. The website provides complete information regarding currently running movies on all screens, including show timings and available seats. Ticket reservations are made using credit cards. Our online ticket reservation system offers convenience for those who cannot afford to wait in long queues. People can book tickets online at any time of day or night.
